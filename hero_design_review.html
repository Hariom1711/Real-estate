This is a premium venture-capital / startup studio aesthetic — liquid glass, cinematic video, character-by-character animation. The design language is excellent. The issue is the original copy and video are built for a VC firm. For a Pune real estate site you need to swap the video, headline, and CTA — the design system itself (glass cards, Inter font, dark cinematic hero) is worth keeping and will make your site stand out from every other Indian real estate site that uses white backgrounds and stock photo sliders.

What works perfectly

  • ✓ Liquid glass navbar — premium, modern, trending in 2026
  • ✓ Full-screen video background — perfect for showing Pune skyline or a project walkthrough
  • ✓ Inter font — clean, readable, very professional for property data
  • ✓ Character animation on heading — memorable first impression
  • ✓ No overlay on video — raw video creates cinematic feel
  • ✓ Glass tag card bottom-right — great for a tagline like "Apartments · Villas · Commercial"
  • ✓ Dark hero with white text — makes property photos on listing cards pop
  • ✓ Fade-in staggered animations — feels crafted, not templated

What needs changing

  • ✗ VC copy ("Shaping tomorrow with vision and action") — wrong audience for property buyers
  • ✗ "Start a Chat" CTA label — should be "Browse Properties" or "Find Your Home"
  • ✗ "Story, Investing, Building, Advisory" nav links — these are VC page names, not real estate
  • ✗ No search bar in hero — the #1 thing buyers expect on a real estate homepage
  • ✗ Original video is abstract / corporate — needs Pune skyline, luxury interiors, or drone footage
  • ! Pure black bg for the rest of site may feel heavy for a 45-min browsing session on listings
  • ! Character animation on every page load could feel slow if user returns often
Bottom line: Keep the entire design system. Swap the copy, video, nav links, and add a search bar below the headline. That's it — the bones are excellent.
1
Replace the video

Use a drone shot of Pune skyline (Baner/Koregaon Park area), a luxury apartment walkthrough, or a sunset aerial. Free source: Pexels.com has good India property videos. Or ask the client if they have any project footage. This is the single biggest impact change.

2
Rewrite the headline

Change "Shaping tomorrow with vision and action" to something like — "Find your place in Pune." or "Premium homes. Prime locations." Keep it short, the character animation works best with under 6 words per line.

3
Add a search bar below the headline

A glass-styled search bar (liquid-glass class) with location dropdown + type dropdown + a search button. This is the #1 conversion driver on real estate sites. Appears after the headline fade-in, at ~1400ms delay. Results go to /listings?location=X&type=Y.

4
Fix the navbar links

Replace "Story, Investing, Building, Advisory" with "Properties, Locations, Compare, Contact". Keep the same glass navbar styling exactly — just different text.

5
Change the bottom-right tag

Replace "Investing. Building. Advisory." with "Apartments · Villas · Commercial" or "Pune's Premium Properties". Same glass card, same animation — just real estate copy.

6
Lighten the rest of the site (not the hero)

Keep the dark cinematic hero for impact. After the user scrolls, switch to a light/neutral background for the listings section — property cards are much easier to read on white/off-white. The contrast between dark hero and light listings section actually looks great.

7
Brand name

Replace "VEX" with your client's brand. The text-2xl font-semibold tracking-tight style is perfect — keep it. Add a small logo image if they have one.
